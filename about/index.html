About Me

I was born in 2004 in Shantou, Guangdong Province, China. I am a typical southern Chinese, which means I have a gentle personality and like to think carefully before doing anything.
I used to love Japanese animation, but now I am more interested in European and American works. I have a very wide range of interests, so I create works with very different styles. I like to explore what I can do.

I think painting expresses more about myself than I can write. So I show some of my own paintings, and my thought process.
